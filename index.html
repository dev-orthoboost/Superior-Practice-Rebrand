Dr. Kishawn Kole-James

Dr. Kole-James has treated patients across the Detroit area for over 8 years. He's board-certified, with a DDS from the University of Detroit Mercy and an orthodontics degree from Howard University.

Before opening Superior Orthodontics, he ran the dental program at three community health clinics, where he learned firsthand what it takes to make good care affordable.

At home, he's outnumbered by his wife Uchenna and their two sons, Samuel and Emmanuel. That same family-first attitude shows up in how he treats every patient.

Common Questions

Straight answers to what patients ask us most.

Yes. Your first visit includes a full exam and digital 3D x-rays, free, with no obligation to start treatment.

It depends on your teeth. Most patients finish in 12 to 24 months, and we'll give you a real timeline at your free exam.
